THE RAMEN HOUSE:

The DOMO ARIGATO Ramen house is a traditional family business founded in 1854 by chef Sensei Kazuho and currently owned in 5th generation by his grand-grand-grand-grand-son Sensei Oshiro.

OUR PHILOSOPHY:

Since the opening about 150 years ago, our philosophy hasn't changed: offering the best ramen. We only use high quality ingredients and traditional family recipies. Our philosophy is based on 3 foundations:

  • finest ingredients
  • cheap prices
  • cooking with love
